About

While the classification is a problem for many things a news classification is one of them because some articles sometimes are related to different topics so in this case, we can use the classifier to classify the article.

This website is a classifier categorizes news articles to five categories. The classifier will accept the article in any language and present the percentage of the relationship of the article to each category.

The classifier was trained with 2225 articles from BBC news. The articles categorized into five categories (business, entertainment, politics, sport, tech).

This website builds by Ahmed Alomar & Ali Alrabeei students in department of Computer Science, Collage of Computer and Information Science, Majmaah University, Al Majmaah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia as a part of a graduating project in 2019.
